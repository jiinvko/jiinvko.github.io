jiinvko, who is otherwise known as ayien among her friends and family,
is a self-taught artist and has always been interested in creating artworks since her grade school years.

Her artworks are mostly inspired from horror, such as junji ito's works and the usual dark, japanese artstyle.
In addition to this, her artstyle mostly consists of "doodle-like" and cyber sigilism inspirations.

As a multimedia artist, she can works on different mediums of art such as woodworking, metalworks, as well as traditional and digital illustrations.
However, she mostly focuses on trad and digital works, most especially 3d chrome works and tattoo designs for clients.
